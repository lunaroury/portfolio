Career arc
Today

I lead at strategic scale.

Senior advisor on digital transformation, AI, and HCD across MENA and globally, counselling governments, donors, and implementing partners on what to build, why, and for whom. The work draws on a decade of practice across sectors, countries, and project realities, ranging from leading the design and delivery of the World Bank's ARTF TPM platform monitoring a $12B+ portfolio in Afghanistan to designing AI-powered policy simulations, mobile-money ecosystems, national job-matching systems, and humanitarian platforms in fragile and conflict-affected contexts.

2016

I shifted into ICT4D.

Souktel was the door. Local became regional. Coordination became leadership. From Project Manager to Chief Program Officer in four years, delivering 15+ digital programmes across 10+ countries, most of them fragile, complex, or in active crisis. The instinct sharpened: technology is only as good as the people it's designed with.

2008–2016

It began in Palestine.

Local coordination roles at the Palestine Stock Exchange, Chemonics, AlSahel, and AMIDEAST. Across finance, healthcare, governance, institutional capacity, and education. The work was small in title, and large in lessons that everything since has been built on.
